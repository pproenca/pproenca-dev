Together with another colleague built a government-certified invoicing platform that started as an internal tool and became a product the company sold. Later, I built the GIS and personalization teams: ML systems powering hyper-local feeds for one of the world's largest classifieds platforms.

I've grown engineers from junior to principal. IC7 at big tech. That might be the work I'm proudest of.
