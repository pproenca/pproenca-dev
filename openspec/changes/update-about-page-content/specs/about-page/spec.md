## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: About Page Content
The about page SHALL display accurate information about the blog author including professional background, technical interests, and contact links.

#### Scenario: Professional background displayed
- **WHEN** a visitor views the about page
- **THEN** they see information about current role at TrustedHousesitters
- **AND** they see mention of engineering leadership experience at Facebook

#### Scenario: Technical achievements highlighted
- **WHEN** a visitor views the about page
- **THEN** they see mention of the smart meetup patent
- **AND** they see areas of technical interest (growth engineering, Python, hardware)

#### Scenario: Contact information available
- **WHEN** a visitor views the about page
- **THEN** they see links to GitHub, LinkedIn, and Twitter profiles
- **AND** the links open in new tabs for external sites
